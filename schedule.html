The Fall 2024 Schedule TBD


The Fall 2024 league season will begin Wednesday, September 11th and continue through October 23rd.

Your team will be scheduled to play 8-10 games over the course of the season, some time between 5:30-9:00p (your games will always be lumped together, expect to play during a roughly 1.5 hour window of time).

The schedule will be released indicating all times/matchups by Team Number (you will receive this after Registration ends). You may show up at 5:30p each week, however, you do not need to arrive until your first matchup begins (it's recommended to come a bit early to warm up, though).
