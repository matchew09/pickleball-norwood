The schedule will also be emailed out via the contact information provided at Registration.

The Fall 2023 league season will begin Wednesday, September 13th and continue through October 18th.

Your team will be scheduled to play 3 games (you'll either play one game, take a break and then play your other two; or play two games, take a break and then play your final game) some time between 5:30-8:30p (your games will always be lumped together, expect to play during a 1.5 hour window of time).

The schedule will be released indicating all times/matchups by Team Number (you will receive this after Registration ends). You may show up at 5:30p each week, however, you do not need to arrive until your first matchup begins (it's recommended to come a bit early to warm up, though).
